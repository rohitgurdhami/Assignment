First, who we are? Angan is operated by Pashupati Foods, and has been engaged in the sweets and snacks endeavor since the past 24 years. Therefore, all preparations are accomplished in accordance with their strict quality control with no compromise. Way back in 1992, Angan was just a traditional sweets, chat, fast food and bakery store. However, it gradually and successfully carried the miracles of taking great traditional taste to new international heights. As a result, it has gained stronghold over the sweet market in Nepal today. Under the supervision of M/s Bikanerwala Foods Pvt. Ltd. (a leading manufacturer of Indian traditional sweets and namkeen) New Delhi, India having more than 59 outlets in and out of India, Angan continues to improve in its every single endeavor achieving new heights of success with every new preparation. Angan is the best place in Nepal for all sweet lovers from the world over to look for. The store offers a whole range of truly traditional sumptuous sweets including laddus, khoya items, dry fruits, sweets, bakery items, Bengali sweets and many more, all 100% vegetarian!
